Welcome to Lindseys Red Dead Redemption 2 Fan Page.

Red Dead Redemption 2 is an action/adventure game developed by Rockstar Games released in 2018.
This is the third release to the Red Dead series, a prequel to the previously released game Red Dead Redemption.

The story is based around the main character and outlaw Arthur Morgan, a member of the Van der Linde gang.
Set in 1899, Arthur struggles to adapt to a changing world with the decline of the wild west, he must attempt
to survive againt goverment officals, rival gangs, and changing times.

You play as Arthur Morgan in an open world set in first and third person perspective. Throughout the story you participate
in shootouts, heists, hunting, interacting with gang members, and much more.

Red Dead Redemption 2 also offer an online option called Red Dead Online. This online multiplayer component allows
you to create a customizable character and freely roam the Red Dead world with other online players.
